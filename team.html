Meet Our Team

Meet the passionate individuals who are driving our mission to protect endangered species and promote sustainability.

Our Teams

At Divture, we believe in working collaboratively to address the most pressing environmental issues. Our teams are composed of dedicated experts who specialize in different aspects of conservation and sustainability. Below, you'll meet the members of our three core teams.

Conservation Team

This team is responsible for researching and developing strategies to protect endangered species, their habitats, and biodiversity worldwide.

Sarah Lee

Conservation Scientist

Sarah is dedicated to studying animal behavior and developing sustainable conservation strategies. She works closely with field teams to track endangered species.

James Miller

Field Conservationist

James spends most of his time in the field, monitoring wildlife populations and helping to implement conservation programs in critical habitats.

Anna Green

Wildlife Biologist

Anna uses scientific methods to study animal populations, with a focus on endangered species in tropical regions. Her research helps guide conservation efforts.

Sustainability Team

This team focuses on promoting sustainable practices and working with communities to develop eco-friendly solutions that benefit both nature and local economies.

David Brown

Sustainability Specialist

David develops strategies to reduce environmental footprints, promoting sustainable farming, waste management, and green energy solutions.

Laura White

Community Outreach Coordinator

Laura works with local communities to foster awareness about sustainable living practices, organizing educational programs and workshops.

Michael Harris

Environmental Policy Advisor

Michael advises on policies that integrate sustainability into local and global frameworks, advocating for environmental responsibility in business and government.

Endangered Animals Specialist Team

This team focuses on providing in-depth knowledge and expertise on endangered species, ensuring they receive the attention they need to avoid extinction.

Mark Johnson

Endangered Species Expert

Mark specializes in rare species and conducts research to identify critical threats to their survival. He is actively involved in global wildlife protection efforts.

Emily Taylor

Wildlife Veterinary Specialist

Emily provides medical care to endangered species, working closely with wildlife sanctuaries to rehabilitate injured animals and ensure their recovery.

Tom Stevens

Ecologist & Wildlife Advocate

Tom works to raise awareness about endangered species and implements ecological restoration projects aimed at safeguarding vulnerable species.
